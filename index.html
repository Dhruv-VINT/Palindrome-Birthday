Palindrome Birthday ! 🍩

Check if your birthdate is palindrome.

A Palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backwards as forward.
Let's find out

Enter Your Birthdate 🍫

This app checks your birthdate in 4 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy,mm-dd-yyyy mm-dd-yy, dd-mm-yy, yy-mm-dd
e.g. So if your birthdate is 4 Aug 2000, then app will check for 04082000, 08042000, 20000804, 040800,080400,000804.
